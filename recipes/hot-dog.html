Hot Dog

Description

Hot dogs are sausages inside a bun

Ingredients

  • Hot dog sausages
  • Hot dog buns
  • Condiments (ketchup, mustard, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop pan.
  2. Place the hot dog sausages on the grill and cook for about 5-7 minutes, turning occasionally, until browned and heated through.
  3. While the sausages are cooking, lightly toast the hot dog buns on the grill.
  4. Assemble the hot dogs by placing each sausage in a bun and adding your favorite condiments.
