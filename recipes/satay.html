Satay Sedap

Description

Satay Chicken is a dish which is originally from Indonesia. In a nutshell, no pun intended, the dish consists of skewered boneless chicken served with peanut sauce. The chicken is traditionally gilled on a hot BBQ, but it can also be cooked on a large non stick fry pan.

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 1 lb/ 500 g chicken thigh fillets(skinless and boneless)
  • 2 1/2 tbsp kecap manis(thisck sweet soy sauce)
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 12-14 small bamboo skewers (soaked in water for at least 30 minutes)

Peanut Sauce

  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 small or 1/2 large onion
  • 3 birds eye chillis
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 1/2 tbsp kecap manis
  • 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup crushed unsalted roasted peanuts
  • 1 -2 tbsp fresh lime juice

Garnish(optional)

  • Crushed peanuts
  • Lime wedges
  • Sliced shallots/scallions
